# Do I need to link my Instagram account to a Facebook page?

To [connect an Instagram account](../settings/connecting-social-media-accounts) in Social Marketing, you must first connect the Instagram account to a Facebook page. 

Users can connect an Instagram account to a Facebook page either through Facebook's Page interface or through Facebook's Business Manager interface.

Please take a look at [this article](../settings/troubleshooting-facebook-instagram-connection-errors) to troubleshoot Facebook and Instagram connections.
