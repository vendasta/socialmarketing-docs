# Do I need to link my Instagram account to a Facebook page?

To [connect an Instagram account](/vendasta-products/social-marketing/connecting-an-instagram-account) in Social Marketing, you must first connect the Instagram account to a Facebook page. 

Users can connect an Instagram account to a Facebook page either through Facebook's Page interface or through Facebook's Business Manager interface.

Please take a look at [this article](/vendasta-products/social-marketing/troubleshooting-facebook-and-instagram-connection-errors) to troubleshoot Facebook and Instagram connections.
