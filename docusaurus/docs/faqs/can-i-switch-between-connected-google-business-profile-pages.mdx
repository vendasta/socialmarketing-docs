# Can I switch between connected Google Business Profile pages?

Yes, you can switch between different Google Business Profile pages that are connected to your Social Marketing account.

To switch between Google Business Profile pages:

1. Go to **Social Marketing > Settings > Connections**
2. Find the Google Business Profile section
3. Click on the page you want to switch to
4. The selected page will become your active Google Business Profile for posting

**Note:** You can only post to one Google Business Profile page at a time, but you can switch between them as needed.
