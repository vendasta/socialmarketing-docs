# Character count limits for social networks

Each social media platform has different character limits for posts:

## Platform Limits

- **Facebook**: 63,206 characters
- **Instagram**: 2,200 characters
- **LinkedIn**: 3,000 characters
- **Twitter/X**: 280 characters
- **Google Business Profile**: 1,500 characters

## LinkedIn Character Count

LinkedIn posts have a 3,000 character limit. When you exceed this limit, the post will be truncated and may not display properly.

**Tip:** Keep your LinkedIn posts concise and engaging to maximize engagement while staying within the character limit.
