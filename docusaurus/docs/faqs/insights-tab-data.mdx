---
title: What data populates in the Insights Tab in Social Marketing?
sidebar_label: Insights Tab Data
description: Learn what data is displayed in the Insights Tab of Social Marketing and how it shows engagement and reach of social media content.
---

Data from the social media content that is posted through Social Marketing will be populated under **Insights**. Users will be able to see the engagement and reach of their social media content over time, as well as a detailed breakdown of the views, reactions, comments, shares (and more) that their posts receive.

**Note:** It will not report on posts made outside the platform.
