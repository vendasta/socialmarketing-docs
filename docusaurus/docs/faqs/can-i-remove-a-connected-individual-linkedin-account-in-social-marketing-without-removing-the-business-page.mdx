# Can I remove a connected individual LinkedIn account in Social Marketing without removing the business page?

Yes, you can remove individual LinkedIn accounts from Social Marketing without affecting your business page connections.

To remove an individual LinkedIn account:

1. Go to **Social Marketing > Settings > Connections**
2. Find the LinkedIn section
3. Click on the individual account you want to remove
4. Select **Remove** or **Disconnect**

This will only remove the specific individual account while keeping your business page connections intact.

**Note:** Make sure you have admin access to the LinkedIn account you're trying to remove.
