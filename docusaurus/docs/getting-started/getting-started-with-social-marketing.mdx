# Getting Started with Social Marketing

## Your One-Stop Solution for Social Media Management

Managing social media can be time-consuming and complex—but it doesn’t have to be. **Social Marketing** offers a powerful, AI-enhanced platform to help businesses of any size grow their online presence, create compelling content, publish across multiple networks, and track success—all from a single dashboard.

## Why Social Marketing?

- Centralized platform for **multi-channel publishing**
- **AI-powered content creation** tools
- Visual **content calendar** and post scheduling
- **Analytics and reporting** for informed decisions
- Collaboration tools for teams
- Pro features to supercharge strategy

## Key Features

### 1. AI-Driven Content Creation

- Generate posts quickly with AI-powered text and images
- Smart captions & hashtag suggestions
- Bulk creation of posts using AI
- Access to pre-built content templates for brand consistency

### 2. Post Management & Scheduling

- **Post Composer**: Write and publish to multiple platforms simultaneously
- **Draft System**: Save and revise content before going live
- **Content Calendar**: Visualize and manage scheduled posts
- **Bulk Scheduling**: Plan and publish large batches of content

### 3. Content Organization & Optimization

- **View past posts**: Reuse and recycle high-performing content
- **Platform-specific optimization**: Tailor each post to the best practices of each platform

### 4. Content Types Supported

- **Text**: Posts, captions, hashtags, mentions
- **Visuals**: Photos, videos, carousels
- **Stories**: Vertical, temporary visual content
- **Link integrations**: Shortened URLs and clickable.bio (Pro)

### 5. Analytics & Reporting

- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, clicks
- **Reach & Impressions**: Know your visibility
- **Audience Growth**: See how your followers evolve
- **Post Performance**: Identify what’s working
- **Export Reports**: CSV download for deeper analysis

> **Note:** Some analytics features require a Social Marketing Pro subscription.

## Best Practices for Success

- **Consistent Branding**: Use templates and brand voice guidelines
- **Content Themes**: Rotate types of content (educational, promotional, entertaining)
- **Use Calls-to-Action**: Invite engagement (“Comment below”, “Visit our site”, etc.)
- **Leverage AI**: Use AI to brainstorm, generate, and refine ideas
- **Optimize Timing**: Use analytics to post when your audience is most active
- **Track What Works**: Learn from top-performing posts to improve future content

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<details>
<summary>Can I delete posts from Social Marketing?</summary>

Yes, for most platforms:

- Facebook, LinkedIn, X, and Google Business Profile: Deleting a post via Social Marketing also deletes it on the platform.
- Instagram: Posts must be deleted directly on Instagram.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I schedule posts in advance?</summary>

Absolutely. Use the **Content Calendar** to plan, draft, and preview your posts before they go live.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I publish to multiple platforms at once?</summary>

Yes. The **Post Composer** allows you to create one post and share it across multiple networks simultaneously.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I track how my content performs?</summary>

Yes. Key performance metrics include:

- Engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Reach and impressions
- Audience growth
- Top-performing content
- CSV report exports
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I collaborate with others on my team?</summary>

Yes. Collaboration tools include:

- Shared Content Calendar
- Draft saving and internal review
- Centralized publishing controls
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Social Marketing use AI for content?</summary>

Yes—especially with a Pro subscription:

- AI post and image generation
- Caption and idea generation
- Bulk content creation
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I run ads through Social Marketing?</summary>

No. Social Marketing is designed for **organic** content only. You'll need to run paid ads directly through the respective platforms.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is A/B testing available?</summary>

Yes. Pro users can test different versions of content to see what performs best.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I export reports?</summary>

Yes. Performance data can be exported in **CSV** format for external reporting or backup.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What's the difference between Standard and Pro again?</summary>

- Standard gives you essential tools for Facebook and Google.
- Pro unlocks full power: more platforms, AI tools, link tracking, unlimited scheduling, deeper analytics, and more.
</details>
